1 What & Why

1.1 Flexible Organization

Sections can be organized however you want, rather than conforming to a rigid hierarchy. They can be embedded within each other, split into separate pages, omitted from table-of-contents listings, all on a section-by-section basis.

1.2 Speed and Safety

Booklit generates static content, rather than running a server. Broken references, malformed documents, and invalid method calls are all caught at compile-time.

1.3 Extensibility with Plugins

Documents are written in a language that can be extended via plugins written in Go. As patterns emerge in your documents, you can optimize for them by quickly writing a plugin and pulling it in. See Extending with Plugins to learn more.
